What it does

Built for creators who prefer control.

🎬

Video Scheduling

Queue up videos to post at precise times. Plan your entire content calendar without being glued to your phone.

⚡

Direct Post API

Uses TikTok's official Content Posting API to publish directly to your profile — no third-party middlemen.

🔐

Secure OAuth Login

Authenticates via TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 flow. Your credentials never leave your machine.

🖥️

Local-First

Runs entirely from your local machine. No cloud subscriptions, no data sharing, no monthly fees.

👤

Multi-Account

Manage multiple TikTok accounts from a single interface. Switch between creators with ease.

📋

Draft Support

Upload videos as drafts for review before they go live, or push them directly to your audience.

Process

Simple by design.

Connect Your Account

Authenticate with TikTok via OAuth. Grant the app permission to post on your behalf.

Upload Your Video

Select your video file, write your caption, configure privacy and engagement settings.

Set Your Schedule

Choose exactly when you want the post to go live, or publish it immediately.

Done

Clever Poster handles the upload and publishes at the right moment. No manual steps.
